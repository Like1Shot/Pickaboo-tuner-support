  # Privacy Policy

  **Last Updated: January 30, 2025**

  ## Introduction

  Pickaboo Tuner ("we," "our," or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains
  how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use our mobile application.

  ## Information We Collect

  ### Information We Do NOT Collect

  - We do not collect personal information (name, email, phone number)
  - We do not collect location data
  - We do not create user accounts
  - We do not store or transmit audio recordings

  ### Microphone Access

  The app requires microphone access to detect the pitch of your instrument. **All audio processing happens entirely 
  on your device in real-time.** Audio data is:
  - Never recorded
  - Never stored
  - Never transmitted to any server

  ### Advertising Data

  We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect certain information automatically, including:

  - Device identifiers (Advertising ID)
  - IP address
  - Device information (model, operating system)
  - Ad interaction data

  This data is collected by Google and is subject to [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

  ## How We Use Information

  - **Audio Input:** Used solely for real-time pitch detection on your device
  - **Advertising Data:** Used by Google AdMob to serve relevant advertisements

  ## Third-Party Services

  ### Google AdMob

  We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob is provided by Google LLC. For more information on how Google
  uses data, please visit:
  - [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
  - [How Google Uses Data](https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites)

  You can opt out of personalized advertising by adjusting your device settings:
  - iOS: Settings > Privacy > Tracking

  ## Children's Privacy

  Pickaboo Tuner is rated 4+ and is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from
  children. The advertisements shown are configured to be appropriate for all audiences.

  ## Data Retention

  Since we do not collect personal data, there is no user data retained on our servers. Any data collected by
  third-party advertising services is subject to their respective retention policies.

  ## Your Rights

  You have the right to:
  - Opt out of personalized advertising through your device settings
  - Request information about data collected by third-party services

  ## Changes to This Policy

  We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy
  Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
