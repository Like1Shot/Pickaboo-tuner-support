# PICKABOO PRIVACY POLICY

**Last updated: Feb 13th, 2026**

Pickaboo ("we", "our", or "the app") is a musical instrument tuner application. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the app.

---

## INFORMATION WE COLLECT

### Audio Data
- The app requests access to your microphone solely to detect pitch for instrument tuning.
- Audio input is processed locally on your device in real time.
- We do **not** record, store, upload, or transmit audio data.

### Advertising
- The app uses Google AdMob to display advertisements.
- AdMob may collect certain information such as device identifiers, IP address, and usage data for advertising purposes.
- For more information, please review Google’s Privacy Policy:  
  https://policies.google.com/privacy

---

## TRACKING

- With your permission, the app may use Apple’s App Tracking Transparency framework to enable personalized ads.
- You can change your tracking preference at any time in your device settings under:  
  **Settings → Privacy → Tracking**

---

## DATA STORAGE

- App preferences (such as tuning settings and sensitivity levels) are stored locally on your device.
- We do not operate backend servers for this app.
- We do not collect or store personal information.

---

## CHILDREN’S PRIVACY

- The app is not directed to children under the age of 13.
- We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
